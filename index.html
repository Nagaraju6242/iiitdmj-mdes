What we have been doing in Corona Crisis

Awards and Achievements

MIT Hack for Future

Hackathon Winner

MIT hack for future is post covid 19 design challenge focusing on Humanizing Artificial Intelligence. The winning team has developed a service ecosystem that helps the healthcare workers and NGOs to record patients’ vitals, identify the severity of the disease and get guided suggestions on the treatment.

MIT Hacking Racism in Healthcare

Hackathon Winner

MIT Racism Hacking in healthcare was an event organized by MIT, a university in Cambridge. In track G Intersectionality, the winning team has developed an app that helps distressed individuals to share their experiences anonymously.

NASA International Space Apps Challenge

Global Award Nominee

NASA’s International Space Apps Challenge is the world’s largest global hackathon, engaging thousands of citizens across the globe to use NASA’s open data to build innovative solutions to challenges we face on Earth and in space. Our students stood out among 26000+ participants across the globe by making an interactive AR prototype.

UXPlorer 2020

Silver Prize Winner

UXplorer Award is among the most unique and desired awards in the UX industry for students from around the world to exhibit their elegant, human-centered product ideas. The winning team designed a product-service system that will ensure proper Diabetes Management in Rural India.

UXIndia DXS 2020

People’s Choice Award Winner Student Award Finalist

The Design X Social Innovation Global Innovation Challenge 2020 is an annual global design award by UX-India. The theme was to design for the new normal. The winning team designed an AR-based repair platform where people can find repair guides from an open-source community and repair expert assistance.

Corona Design Challenge

5 Award Winning Teams

During the corona crisis, 5 teams were selected as winning entries from the MDes’19 batch in D’source Corona Design Challenge conducted by IIT Bombay. The winning teams are from various track product, UI/UX, Visual.

Yanko Design

Featured Projects in Industrial Design

One product named "TOCO" designed by our student got featured on the Instagram Page of Yanko Design. Yanko Design is an award-winning publication and home of product design and innovation focusing on pleasurable products.

Behance.net

Featured Project in Industrial Design

One project was featured on the Behance in Industrial Design Segment. The featured title is NOPS - No Passive Smoking. It is a wearable air purifier primarily aimed at non-smokers to prevent second-hand smoking. This can be also used as a replacement for a pollution mask

BIRAC Fellowship

2 Students Selected

BIRAC's SRISTI INNOVATION AWARD Scheme is aimed at supporting innovative student projects in the area of the Medical Equipment & Healthcare Devices field. The scheme provides an opportunity for orientation, training and mentoring through residential workshops. One student from our batch was selected for the same.

SH Innovation Think Tank 2020

Winner - 3rd Place

Our students mapped out opportunities for designing hospitals of the future and how advanced technologies can be integrated into the core competencies of a hospital to realize a true place of healing.

Typo Day 2020

Logo Design Selected

The Typoday is an annual event organized by IIT Bombay. This was the winning Entry, It was elected by the jury members for its high quality of representation.

IIC MHRD Innovation contest

Finalist

The Ministry of Education (MoE) had announced the score 95/100 for the Performance Rating of Institutions’ Innovation Council. Many Design student’s contributions were there to achieve the same.
